Introductions

What are we doing?

    When it comes to constantly change school time lines and working part time jobs, scheduling can quickly become a difficult and tedious problem for many college students or working adults. We are building an online web-service calendar application that allows people to plan their schedules ahead of time with ease in an attempt to combat issues of figuring out availability and overall time management.


Functionality Goals:

    Display a weekly calendar, a user would be able to add events to the calendar, remove events, and save their personalized calendar to a server and retrieve it at any time.
